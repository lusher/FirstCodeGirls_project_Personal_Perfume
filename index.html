Woody

Woody scents are based on bark and moss notes that conjure images of winding forest paths.Generate a strong classic apeal, ideal for corporate atmosphere and business meetings.

Oriental

These sensual blends are exotic and distinctly feminine, featuring an earthy, animal-like based scent like musk or ambergris, often combined with warm notes like amber.
