Products

Floral

Our most popular choice of perfume with romantic, light and sweet scents made of a blend of flowers such as lily, jasmine, rose and lavender.

Spicy

Spicy notes such as cinnamon, cloves and nutmegfor warmth, sensuality and depth.

Fruity

Feel fresh and healthy with pleasing familiar notes of apple, berry,lime and coconat.

Ocean

Feel natural aroma of mountain air, sea spray and blue oceans.

Woody

Woody scents are based on bark and moss notes that conjure winding forest paths.Generate a strong classic apeal, ideal for corporate atmosphere.

Oriental

These sensual blends are exotic and distinctly feminine, featuring an earthy, animal-like base scent like musk or ambergris, often combined with warm notes like amber. These fragrances are called "florientals" when combined with florals.
